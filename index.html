ALWAYS CLICK "MINT BADGE"

Before taking any steps, check if you qualify to mint a badge (this might sometimes happen due to your past activity).

REVOKE OFTEN

When earning badges, we often interact with less-known projects. For your own safety, after completing a series of tasks for multiple badges, revoke all permissions in your wallet (for example, using "Batch Revoke" in the Rabby wallet under the "Approvals" icon). The cost is negligible.

ERRORS AND ISSUES

Newly added badges often have various bugs. Even after completing the tasks, you might see that you're not eligible. Sometimes, even if you are eligible, an error message may appear during minting. These are common occurrences that are gradually resolved by Scroll and the teams responsible for the badges. Be patient.

TEMPORARILY UNAVAILABLE BADGES

Some badges on the list are marked with an orange border. These are badges that were added to the official Scroll list and then withdrawn, mainly for technical reasons. Most of these badges are re-added to the list under a new contract. When this happens, it is updated on this list (including links).

HOW THIS LIST WORKS

The list uses scripts to force your browser to remember each of your actions. Each check or uncheck of a checkbox is immediately saved and is not affected by a refresh or sudden browser closure. Simultaneously, every change immediately generates a special "memory code" that you should copy and save somewhere because if you accidentally click the RESET button and erase your list's state (or manually clear your browser memory/cache), you'll be able to recover it by pasting your code and clicking the ENTER button. I don't recommend using the list in incognito mode because such browsers clear memory every time they're restarted. For your convenience, use the list in a browser that saves events.

YOU CAN HELP ME

If you notice that a link to a badge isn't working, it means it has been removed from the Scroll list. Let me know about it on our Discord so I can update the corresponding badge. Also, if you notice any other issues with the list, please inform me. We're creating this list together.
